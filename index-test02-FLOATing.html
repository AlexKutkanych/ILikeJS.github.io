  5. Each rectangle goes one by another.
  6. There are 3 containers where I locate all rectangles.
  7. Those containers differ by their WIDTH-size !
  8. There are also 2 tests of FLOATing where the 5th rectangle either TOP-margined or TOP&Left margined.
